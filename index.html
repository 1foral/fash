About

My name is Junior Musheni and I am a 22-year-old Congolese fashion artist and creator of PROVOCATION. I have always been passionate about designing my own clothes and wearing my creations, as well as everything related to fashion. I love playing with styles, combining different pieces, and creating unique looks that are both innovative and visually pleasing. For me, fashion is a form of expression where every outfit tells a story and reflects an identity. I don't just follow trends, I reinvent them in my own way, mixing textures, colors, and diverse inspirations. My goal is to create fashion compositions that leave a lasting impression and give everyone the freedom to boldly and creatively express their style.
